About us

IASAT is an assessment test conducted by P&R Pvt Ltd’’ India & Australia in association with various universities across worldwide to provide a platform for intellectual and creative students who are willing to pursue their higher education overseas. At P&R Pvt Ltd’ we are committed to provide best tailor-made education & Training. Our world class trainers know the value of real time education. We believe education and knowledge are the most powerful weapons one can have regardless of being rich or poor, weak or strong, all one needs is the treasure of knowledge to defeat and overcome the problems of your life!
